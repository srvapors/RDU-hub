The Foundational Principles

A Timeless, State-Based Cosmos

[span_0](start_span)

The Resonant Dark Universe is built upon a single, foundational postulate: the universe is fundamentally timeless[span_0](end_span). [span_1](start_span)In this framework, time is not a dimension but an emergent property of causality[span_1](end_span). [span_2](start_span)Reality is described as a discrete, causally-ordered sequence of quantum states, and physical laws are reconceptualized as transition rules that govern the progression from one state to the next[span_2](end_span). [span_3](start_span)This progression is enforced by the finite propagation speed of interactions through the Chronos Field[span_3](end_span).

The Origin of the Dark Sector

The Black Hole-DRC Identity

[span_8](start_span)

A foundational axiom of the RDU is the Black Hole-DRC Identity, which posits that all black holes are active Dark Resonator Cores (DRCs)[span_8](end_span). [span_9](start_span)These objects are the exclusive sites where matter is compressed with enough force to cause a localized return to the original Unified Field state[span_9](end_span).

The Knoechelman Mechanism

[span_10](start_span)

Through the Knoechelman Mechanism, DRCs perform a gravitational crunch that transforms infalling baryonic matter into a spectrum of stable, superluminal particles called Aumons[span_10](end_span). [span_11](start_span)[span_12](start_span)These Aumons constitute the dark sector, providing a direct, physical origin for both dark matter and dark energy from known astrophysical objects[span_11](end_span)[span_12](end_span).
